# Astral Frontier - Quick Reference Card

## üöÄ Ship Builder

### Access
**Press H** - Open Ship Builder (from game)

### Controls
- **WASD** - Move part forward/left/back/right
- **Space/Shift** - Move part up/down
- **Left Click** - Place part
- **Right Click** - Remove last part
- **Middle Mouse + Drag** - Rotate camera
- **Mouse Wheel** - Zoom camera
- **ESC** - Exit builder

### Quick Actions
- **Presets Button** - Load pre-built designs (Fighter, Interceptor, etc.)
- **Clear Button** - Remove all parts
- **Save Button** - Save current design
- **Apply Button** - Use design on your ship
- **Exit Button** - Return to game

### Tips
- Start with a preset, then modify
- Keep T/W ratio above 0.5 for good flight
- Mirror parts on both sides for balance
- Check stats panel on right for real-time feedback

## üõ†Ô∏è Ship Stats Explained

### T/W Ratio (Thrust-to-Weight)
- **Below 0.3** - Won't fly well
- **0.3-0.5** - Sluggish but flyable
- **0.5-1.0** - Good performance ‚≠ê
- **1.0+** - Excellent acceleration
- **1.5+** - Fighter jet performance

### Mass
- Lower = Faster, more agile
- Higher = Slower, more stable
- Every part adds mass

### Hull
- Structural damage capacity
- Add more hull parts to increase

### Shields
- Regenerating energy barrier
- Add Shield Generator to increase

### Thrust
- Engine power output
- More engines = more thrust

### Fuel
- Operational range
- Add Fuel Tanks to increase

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Mouse Stuck in Ship Builder
- **Restart game** - Changes require compilation
- **Check**: Mouse should unlock when H pressed

### Low FPS on Planet
- **Press F4** - Toggle to low quality
- **Reduces objects** from 500 to 250

### Ship Won't Move
- **Check T/W ratio** - Must be above 0.3
- **Add more engines** or remove parts

### Ship Spins Uncontrollably
- **Balance issue** - Mirror parts on both sides
- **Rebuild in Ship Builder** with symmetry

### Weather Not Visible
- **Wait 30-90 seconds** - Weather changes automatically
- **Press F2** - Check current weather status

### Can't Place Parts in Builder
1. **Select a part** from left panel
2. **Position with WASD**
3. **Left click to place**
4. **Make sure not clicking on UI panels**

## üéØ Quick Start Guide

### First Time Playing
1. **Launch game** - Game starts in space
2. **Fly around** - WASD + Mouse
3. **Press H** - Try ship builder
4. **Load Fighter preset** - Good starting ship
5. **Press L** - Land on planet
6. **Explore** - Walk around, see weather
7. **Press F3** - Cycle time of day
8. **Have fun!**

### Building Your First Custom Ship
1. **Press H** in game
2. **Click "Clear All"**
3. **Add Cockpit** (center)
4. **Add Hull Forward** (behind cockpit)
5. **Add 2x Swept Wings** (left and right)
6. **Add 2x Medium Engines** (back)
7. **Check T/W > 0.5**
8. **Click "Apply to Ship"**
9. **Press ESC, test fly!**

### Exploring Your First Planet
1. **Fly near planet** in space
2. **Press L** to land
3. **WASD to walk** around
4. **Look for objects** - rocks, trees, plants
5. **Watch weather** - changes automatically
6. **Press F3** - See day and night
7. **Press F4** - Try quality modes
8. **Press ESC** - Return to space
